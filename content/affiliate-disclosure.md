At **Practivio**, we believe transparency builds trust.

We research and recommend products to help you make smart, informed buying decisions. Some of the links on our site are referral links — if you click and purchase, we may earn a small commission. This comes at **no extra cost to you**.

These commissions help us:

- Keep Practivio clean, fast, and ad-free  
- Support independent editorial work  
- Test and compare new products  
- Publish in-depth reviews and insights regularly  

We never accept payment to recommend or favor any brand. Our choices are based on product performance, value, and relevance to you.

Have questions? Want us to review something? [Contact us](mailto:affiliates@practivio.com).
